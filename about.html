About Us

Neil Brozen

CPA since 1981, when he joined Arthur Anderson, then the largest accounting firm in the world. He was hired away by a client, Marquette Bank. For 8 years, Neil was the problem solver for over 30 community banks in 8 states. When a banker had a issue, from a drive through building demolished by a tornado to a regulatory investigation, they called Neil.


Neil took his broad business experience to his wn management consulting company. He analyzed business issues, did research and designed new programs and systems. Clients included small businesses, large non-profits and fortune 500 corporations.


In 2003 Neil joined BNC, a multi-state community bank. After serving as CFO, he was asked to start a new ESOP trustee practice. This introduced Neil to the field he has enjoyed more than any other professional experience. In 2010 Neil moved to Bankers Trust where he tripled his book of business over the next 6 years. Additionally he led Bankers Trust ESOP Services on a huge growth curve, from $700k annually before he arrived, to $4.2 million.
